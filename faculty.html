GILBERT D. DEBOMA, REE, M.Eng.

Engr. Gilbert D. Deboma has been a faculty member of the College of Engineering for 24 years, holding the position of Associate Professor V. He earned his Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from Technological Institute of the Philippines – Quezon City and his Master of Science in Electronics Engineering from Andong National University in South Korea. Currently pursuing a PhD in Agricultural Engineering at Central Luzon State University, he is also an active member of the Institute of Integrated Electrical Engineers, Inc., the recognized professional organization for electrical practitioners in the Philippines. In addition to his teaching role, he serves as the dean of the College of Engineering and the Program Chairperson of the Electrical Engineering Department.

MELOJEAN C. MARAVE, LPT, MSIT

Engr. Melojean C. Marave boasts a robust educational background, including a Doctorate in Information Technology from Technological Institute of the Philippines and a Doctor of Education with a focus on Educational Management from Ramon Magsaysay Technological University, Iba Campus. She also holds a Master of Science in Information Technology from Polytechnic University of the Philippines and a Post-Baccalaureate Certificate in Professional Education from Ramon Magsaysay Technological University Main Campus. Additionally, she completed a Trade Course in IT Course Design and Development and CISCO Certified Network Associate modules from UP Information Technology Training Center. She earned her Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering in 2001. Currently, Engr. Marave serves as the BS Computer Engineering Program Chairperson at PRMSU Iba, Zambales, and is actively engaged in various faculty and extension roles, particularly in community extension. She is a licensed professional teacher and a member of several professional organizations, including Colleges and Universities in the Philippines and the Institute of Computer Engineers of the Philippines. She also serves as an Accreditor for AACCUP and an Internal Auditor for ISO, and has been recognized for her contributions to youth leadership and leadership excellence.

MARLON JAMES A. DEDICATORIA, RMEE, Ph.D.

Dr. Marlon James A. Dedicatoria is a licensed mechanical engineer serving as the Vice President for Research and Development and Chairperson of the Mechanical Engineering Program at President Ramon Magsaysay State University (PRMSU) in Iba, Zambales. With over a decade of teaching experience, he instructs courses in the Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering (BSME) program and teaches mathematics at the graduate school level. He earned his Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Ramon Magsaysay Technological University in 2006, followed by a Master of Science and a PhD in Mechanical Design and Systems Engineering from Andong National University, South Korea. He also completed a post-doctoral fellowship at Andong National University’s Materials Behavior Evaluation Laboratory. His research focuses on the electromechanical properties of high-temperature superconductors. Dr. Dedicatoria currently holds the rank of Professor 3 at PRMSU.
